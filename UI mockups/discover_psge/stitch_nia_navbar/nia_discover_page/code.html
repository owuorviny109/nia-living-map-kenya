Maasai Mara National Reserve

Narok County

Experience the iconic Great Migration in this vast savanna, home to lions, elephants, and rhinos. A premier safari destination offering unparalleled wildlife viewing.

Diani Beach

Kwale County

Relax on pristine white sands and enjoy turquoise waters. This idyllic coastal paradise is perfect for water sports, relaxation, and vibrant nightlife.

Amboseli National Park

Kajiado County

Famous for its large elephant herds and breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro across the border in Tanzania. A photographer's dream.

Lamu Old Town

Lamu County

Explore the oldest and best-preserved Swahili settlement in East Africa. A UNESCO World Heritage site with narrow streets, stone houses, and a unique culture.

Mount Kenya

Meru County

Challenge yourself by climbing Africa's second-highest peak. This extinct volcano offers diverse ecosystems, from dense forests to alpine meadows.

Tsavo East National Park

Taita-Taveta County

Discover the 'Land of Lava, Springs & Magical Sunsets.' This vast park is known for its large elephant herds and the legendary man-eating lions of Tsavo.
